Game Rules:

  • The goal of the game: The goal is to find and destroy all of your opponent's ships before they destroy yours.

  • Ships: Each player has a set of five ships of different lengths:
    • 1 ship of 5 squares (Aircraft Carrier)

    • 1 ship of 4 squares (Battleship)

    • 1 ship of 3 squares (Cruiser)

    • 1 ship of 3 squares (Submarine)

    • 1 ship of 2 squares (Destroyer)
  • If a player hits an opponentâ€™s ship, the block turns red, indicating they have hit part of a ship.

  • Each player has a time limit of 5 to 20 seconds per turn.

  • To sink a ship, the player must hit every square of the ship.

  • The first player to sink all their opponent's ships wins the game.

  • Note: Ships may not be placed diagonally or overlap each other.

Good luck!
